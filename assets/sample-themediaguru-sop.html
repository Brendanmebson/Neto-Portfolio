Content Production Workflow
Step-by-step process from brief to first draft
01
Client Brief Received
Client submits a completed brief via the standard intake form. Account manager reviews and flags any missing information within 2 hours.
Brief must include: brand voice, target audience, platform, and CTA
Content assigned to writer within 2 hours of receipt
NotionGoogle FormsSlack
02
Research & Strategy
Writer reviews brand guidelines, past content, competitor landscape, and trending topics before writing.
Review brand style guide stored in Notion
Identify 3–5 relevant trends or angles before drafting
NotionGoogle TrendsMeta Business Suite
03
Draft Creation
Writer produces first draft following the brand voice guide. Draft includes copy, caption, hashtag recommendations, and visual direction notes.
Google DocsCanvaNotion
04
Internal Quality Review
Senior editor reviews draft against brand guidelines, grammar, tone, and strategic alignment. Completed within 4 hours.
GrammarlyGoogle Docs
05
Client Submission & Approval
Approved draft sent to client via shared content calendar. Client has 24 hours to approve or request changes.
Google DriveLater.comSlack
06
Publish & Report
Once approved, content is scheduled. Performance data captured at 24h, 72h, and 7-day marks.
BufferMeta BusinessGoogle Analytics

4.0
QA Checklist — Before Client Submission
Every piece of content must pass this checklist
Brand Voice: Copy matches the client's approved tone and style guide
Hook: First sentence is compelling enough to stop a scroll
CTA: Clear call-to-action aligned with campaign objective
Grammar & Spelling: Proofread with Grammarly Premium; zero errors allowed
Platform Optimised: Format, length, and hashtags appropriate for the platform
Links/Tags: All hyperlinks, profile tags, and mentions are verified and active
Legal/Compliance: No copyright infringement or false claims
